I’m Baraa Khatib Salkini

Since 2008, I’ve been working with data.
What started as a curiosity during my student days turned into a full career — from junior developer to consultant, to lead engineer, and now product owner leading a global team of data engineers, analysts, and scientists.

Over the years, I’ve helped build and deliver large-scale data platforms for some of Germany’s biggest companies — including Mercedes-Benz and Bosch.
But honestly, what I’ve always enjoyed most isn’t the code or the systems — it’s explaining how things work.
I love taking something complex and breaking it down visually.

That’s how Data With Baraa began — The mission has always been the same: to teach data not as syntax or tools, but as a way of thinking — and seeing learners build real projects or land their first data jobs because of it is the best feeling in the world.

If you’ve watched a video, shared a sketch, or learned something from me — thank you.
This journey wouldn’t exist without you.
And honestly, this is still just the beginning.
